    Our Mission: Better Sleep

    For decades, only the most serious sleep aficionados have known this secret: natural latex is simply the best sleep surface there is.

    Perhaps as a result, mattress companies have overcharged for natural latex mattresses with markups so high that only the lucky few were able to enjoy them. We think that’s wrong and we’re here to change it.

    Latex for Less has cut out the middlemen, partnering directly with farmers to provide you with a single-source natural latex mattress at the most affordable price possible.

    We Partner Directly With Farmers

    When other mattress companies buy their materials on the secondary market, they end up paying extra. We knew there had to be a better way. We sought out the best farms and formed sustainable partnerships directly with farmers. Not only does that eliminate unnecessary markups, but it allows you to know exactly what you’re sleeping on.

    We Handcraft In-House

    Many companies outsource their production to large manufacturers. This can result in less attention to detail and a lower quality product. Companies who outsource can also can lose control over how well workers are treated and how the process impacts the environment. That doesn’t work for us.

    We handcraft each mattress ourselves, here in America. By investing in production, we keep jobs here at home, while minimizing our emissions. We’ve also reduced costs, but with innovation, not shortcuts.

    We Thought of Everything

    After over a decade of research, we’ve found some pretty obvious problems with the way other companies make mattresses. By providing you with two firmness levels in one mattress, you can choose the one that’s best for you right out of the box. And if your body’s needs change over time, you’re never more than a flip away from a better firmness level for you.
